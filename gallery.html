Architecture

Bafana Hotel Exterior

Designed with modern architectural elegance, Bafana Hotel International stands as a beacon of hospitality in Kaduna. Enjoy secure parking, beautiful facades, and a warm welcome from the moment you arrive.

Welcome

Reception & Lounge

Our grand lobby and reception area offer 24/7 guest service with express check-in. Relax in our luxurious lobby waiting area designed with comfort and elegance in mind.

Accommodations

Luxury Rooms & Suites

Unwind in style with our thoughtfully decorated rooms and master suites. Outfitted with premium bedding, climate control, high-speed Wi-Fi, and plush interior finishes.

Fine Dining

Main Restaurant

Savor a fusion of local Nigerian delicacies and international cuisine, prepared fresh by expert chefs in an ambiance designed for memorable meal gatherings.

Nightlife & Drinks

Executive Bar

Kick back and enjoy crafted cocktails, ice-cold beverages, and soft ambient lighting at our fully stocked bar. A perfect venue to socialize or unwind after a busy day.

Recreation

Outdoor Swimming Pool

Take a refreshing dip in our crystal-clear pool or soak up the sun on comfortable poolside loungers. Ideal for morning swims or relaxing evening poolside drinks.

Events & Corporate

Event & Conference Hall

Host your weddings, corporate meetings, and celebrations in our fully equipped, spacious event hall featuring modern lighting, sound systems, and air conditioning.

Outdoors

Open-Air Courtyard

Enjoy outdoor dining, evening banter, or breezy night gatherings in our spacious, well-lit outdoor grounds designed for fresh air relaxation.

Family Friendly

Children's Playground

Keep your young ones entertained in our safe, fun-filled playground. Complete with slides, rides, and supervised play spaces for maximum family convenience.

Grooming

Barbing Salon

Look and feel your best without leaving the hotel premises. Our professional barbing salon offers expert haircuts, styling, and grooming services.

Convenience

Provision Store & POS

Grab essential snacks, toiletries, travel necessities, and cash services conveniently right inside our complex at the onsite provision store and POS station.
